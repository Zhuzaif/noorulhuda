Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23 Feb 2026

Welcome to Noor Ul Huda Kids. Protecting children's privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

1. Children's Privacy

Noor Ul Huda Kids is designed for children aged 5–15 under parental guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

2. Information We Collect

Our app may collect limited information to improve the learning experience.

This may include:

  • Student nickname (optional)
  • Avatar customization data
  • Learning progress and level progress
  • Stars, coins, achievements, and badges
  • Quiz statistics such as accuracy, correct answers, questions answered, etc.
  • Daily learning streaks and activity tracking
  • Salah tracking progress (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
  • Anonymous usage analytics and app performance data

This information is used only to track learning progress, improve the educational experience, and personalize the app features.

Images Policy: If the app displays or loads images, they may be stored temporarily in the device's local cache to improve performance. These images are not stored in our database and are not uploaded to our servers.

We do NOT collect:

  • Real names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location
  • Contacts or personal photos

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve learning features
  • Save progress, rewards, and achievements
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

4. Third-Party Services

Noor Ul Huda Kids may use trusted services such as Firebase Analytics and crash reporting tools to improve app performance. These services may collect anonymous technical data.

5. Ads Policy

If advertisements are used in the future, they will be child-friendly and compliant with Google Play Families Policy. We do not serve personalized ads to children.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures including secure servers and encrypted connections. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Parental Rights

Parents/guardians may:

  • Request to review their child's data
  • Request deletion of data
  • Contact us for privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.
